Project description

Introducing my freelance portal project, crafted with Angular, Node.js, Express, and MySQL.

This dynamic platform serves as a bridge between clients seeking skilled freelancers and professionals eager to showcase their talents.

Featuring a clean and intuitive user interface, clients can effortlessly post work opportunities, specifying their requirements and expectations. Freelancers, on the other hand, can easily browse available projects, apply for suitable opportunities, and showcase their portfolios.
